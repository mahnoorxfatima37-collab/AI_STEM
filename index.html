About Jennie & Ruby

Jennie debuted as a member of BLACKPINK with the iconic double title tracks Boombayah and Whistle. In November 2018, she made her solo debut with SOLO, a massive commercial success that showcased her versatility as both a singer and rapper. The song broke numerous records and earned multiple awards.

On March 7, 2025, Jennie released her debut solo album Ruby. The album highlights her diverse artistry, blending confident hip-hop, catchy pop, sultry R&B, and introspective emotional tones.

Record-Breaking Achievements

  • Over 1 million first-week sales — highest for a K-pop female soloist in 2025.
  • Surpassed 4.8 million global sales — best-selling K-pop album of 2025.
  • Highest-charting K-pop female solo album on the UK Albums Chart (#3).
  • First K-pop female solo album certified Silver by the BPI.
  • Multiple Billboard Hot 100 entries — record for a female K-pop solo album.
  • Mantra became the highest-charting UK single by a Korean female soloist.
  • Most certified K-pop female soloist in several markets.
  • Over 2.5 billion Spotify streams — most for any 2025 K-pop act.

Major Awards

  • Billboard Women in Music 2025 — Global Force Award.
  • 40th Golden Disc Awards (2026) — Artist of the Year (Daesang), Best Digital Song, Global Impact Award.
  • 2025 Melon Music Awards — Record of the Year (Daesang), Top 10 Artist, Millions Top 10.
  • China Year-End Awards — Swept 4 major categories.
  • RIAA Gold Certifications for “like JENNIE” and “Mantra”.
  • Featured in best-of-2025 lists by Rolling Stone, Billboard, and Pitchfork.
